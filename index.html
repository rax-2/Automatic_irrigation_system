Automatic irrigation system

Introduction

The Automatic Irrigation System is designed to ensure plants get the right amount of water by checking the soil's moisture levels. This project uses an Arduino Uno, a SEN6 soil moisture sensor, a DC water pump, and a relay. The Arduino checks the soil moisture, and if the soil is too dry, it turns on the water pump to water the soil.

Objectives

Automate the watering of plants based on soil moisture.

Reduce water wastage.

Minimize the need for manual watering.

Components

Arduino Uno: Controls the system.

SEN6 Soil Moisture Sensor: Measures soil moisture.

DC Water Pump: Pumps water to the soil.

Relay Module: Switches the water pump on and off.

Conclusion

This project shows that we can use simple electronic components and an Arduino Uno to automate irrigation. The system works well to keep soil moisture at the right levels, reducing the need for manual watering.
